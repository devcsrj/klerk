CALL TO ORDER


At 3:00 p.m., the Senate President, Hon. Vicente
C. Sotto III, called the session to order.


PRAYER


Sen. Maria Lourdes Nancy S. Binay led the
prayer, to wit:


Let us put ourselves in the presence of our
Lord.


Today, we are reminded by the words
in Proverbs 107, verse 28:


“Then they cried out to the Lord in
their trouble, and he brought them out of
their distress.”


Our Father,


Bless us all here present in the halls of
the Senate. Bless us with Your divine
wisdom and bounty so that we can help and



support each other, as we work to make our
country a better place.


Dalangin po namin ang mabilis na
pagbangon ng aming mga kapatid mula sa
mga kalamidad na bumisita sa aming
bayan sa nakalipas na mga araw. We
remember Your covenant with Your people,
na hindi po Ninyo pababayaan ang Inyong
faongbayan at ililigtas kami sa kapa-
hamakan.




Gawin Mo po kaming tanglaw at
pag-asa ng aming mga kababayan,
Jalung-lalo na ‘yong nalubog ang mga
bahay at kabuhayan sa patuloy na pag-
uulan at pagbabaha sa iba ‘ibang panig
ng bansa. Let us be their solace and
comfort in these trying times.


These we pray to You our Lord, our
Guide and Savior.


Amen.
