LEGISLATIVE CALENDAR
EIGHTEENTH CONGRESS, FIRST REGULAR SESSON

Adopted from House Concurrent Resolution No. 93

Introduced by Rep. Ferdinand Martin G. Romualdez and Rep. Bienvenido M. Abante, Jr.

CONCURRENT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR THE LEGISLATIVE CALENDAR FOR THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE EIGHTEENTH CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES.

RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, THE SENATE CONCURRING, That the Legislative Calendar for the First Regular Session of the Eighteenth Congress of the Philippines shall be as follows:

 
1. Commencement of Session July 22, 2019 - October 4, 2019
2. Adjournment of Session October 5, 2019 - November 3, 2019
3. Resumption of Session November 4, 2019 - December 20, 2019
4. Adjournment of Session December 21, 2019 - January 19, 2020
5. Resumption of Session January 20, 2020 - March 13, 2020
6. Adjournment of Session March 14, 2020 - May 3, 2020
7. Resumption of Session May 4, 2020 - June 5, 2020
(sine die adjournment)
8. Adjournment of Session June 6, 2020 - July 26, 2020
